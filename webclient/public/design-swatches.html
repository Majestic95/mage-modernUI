Elimination slash — keep-with-slash overlay

Per ADR D2, eliminated players' pods stay visible with a paired --color-eliminated-slash red fill + --color-eliminated-slash-outline white outline so the diagonal-slash shape signals elimination under all forms of color blindness.

Defeated foe
--color-eliminated-slash: #E5483D
--color-eliminated-slash-outline: #FFFFFF
Slash stroke ≥4px; white outline below red fill so the diagonal shape persists under deuteranopia/protanopia/tritanopia where the red collapses to grey.

Status — semantic state

Reuses the mana palette where the semantics align (success = green, danger = red, warning = gold, info = blue) — keeps the system coherent and saves 4 distinct hex picks.

Success +5 life Danger -3 life Time pressure Phase Update
--color-status-success: #5BB872 (mirrors --color-mana-green)
--color-status-danger: #E5483D (mirrors --color-eliminated-slash)
--color-status-warning: #E8C56C (mirrors --color-mana-multicolor)
--color-status-info: #5BC0F0 (mirrors --color-mana-blue)
